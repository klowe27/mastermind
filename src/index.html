Mastermind

Welcome to Mastermind!

In this game the computer has generated a 4 color pattern that you have to guess in the amount of turns given.

Once you have made your first guess, the pegs in the right hand box fill with one of 3 colors:

  • Gold Peg: You have an exact match (correct color and postion).
  • White Peg: You have a color match (color but not position).
  • Gray: Your guess is 100% wrong. Yikes.

Win: You guess the correct parttern before the turn limit.

Lose: You hit the guess limit before guessing the pattern correctly.

Select 4 colors to submit.

Congratulations!
